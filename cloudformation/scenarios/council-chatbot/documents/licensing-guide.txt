SAMPLE COUNCIL - LICENSING GUIDE

Premises Licence (Alcohol and Entertainment)
You need a premises licence to sell alcohol, provide late-night refreshment, or hold regulated entertainment.
Application fee: 100-1,905 pounds depending on rateable value.
Apply to: licensing@samplecouncil.gov.uk with plans, DPS consent, and operating schedule.
Processing time: 28 days consultation plus 2 weeks for decision.

Personal Licence
Required for anyone authorising the sale of alcohol. You must hold a licensing qualification.
Fee: 37 pounds. Apply with your qualification certificate and basic DBS check.

Taxi and Private Hire Licensing
Driver licence: Apply with DBS enhanced check, medical certificate, driving record, and knowledge test. Fee: 250 pounds (3 years).
Vehicle licence: Annual inspection required. Fee: 200 pounds per year.
Operator licence: For private hire operators. Fee: 500 pounds (5 years).
Apply: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/taxi-licensing

Street Trading
You need a consent to trade on the street. Designated consent streets are listed on our website.
Daily consent: 25 pounds. Annual consent: 1,500 pounds.
Apply at least 28 days before you wish to start trading.

Animal Licensing
Licences required for: dog breeding (3+ litters/year), pet shops, animal boarding, riding schools, dangerous wild animals.
Fee: 150-400 pounds depending on licence type. Inspections required before licence granted.

Contact: licensing@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-897
