SAMPLE COUNCIL - WASTE AND RECYCLING FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: My bin wasn't collected today - what should I do?
A: Report it within 2 working days at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/missed-collection or call 01234-567-890. We will return within 3 working days. Please leave your bin out until collected.

Q: How do I get a new or replacement bin?
A: Order online at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/new-bin. Delivery takes 10 working days. Cost: 25 pounds for a replacement black or blue bin. Green bins are free when you sign up for garden waste.

Q: Can I put extra rubbish out beside my bin?
A: No, we only collect waste from the wheeled bin. Extra bags left beside the bin will not be collected. If you regularly have too much waste, consider a larger bin (available for households with 6 or more residents).

Q: What do I do with old batteries?
A: Place batteries in a small clear bag on top of your blue recycling bin on collection day. Do not put them loose inside the bin.

Q: My recycling bin was rejected - why?
A: Common reasons include food-contaminated items, plastic bags, nappies, or textiles in the blue bin. Remove the contamination and present the bin on your next collection day.

Contact: waste@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-890
