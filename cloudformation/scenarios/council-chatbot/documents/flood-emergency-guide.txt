SAMPLE COUNCIL - FLOOD EMERGENCY INFORMATION

Flood Risk
Parts of the borough are at risk of flooding from rivers and surface water. Check your flood risk at www.gov.uk/check-flood-risk.

Before a Flood
Sign up for free flood warnings from the Environment Agency: www.gov.uk/sign-up-for-flood-warnings or call 0345 988 1188.
Prepare a flood kit: torch, phone charger, medication, important documents, insurance details.
Know how to turn off gas, electricity, and water.

During a Flood
Call 999 if there is a danger to life.
Do not walk or drive through flood water (15cm can knock you over, 30cm can move a car).
Move to higher ground if advised.
Council emergency line: 01234-567-999.

After a Flood
Do not return to your property until advised it is safe.
Report flood damage: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/flood-report or 01234-567-895.
We can provide sandbags for future protection (limited availability, priority given to vulnerable residents).
Council Tax discount may be available for uninhabitable properties. Contact 01234-567-891.

Contact: flooding@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-895
