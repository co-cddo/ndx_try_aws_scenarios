SAMPLE COUNCIL - PLANNING APPLICATIONS GUIDE

Do I Need Planning Permission?
You need planning permission for most building work, changes of use, and alterations that affect the external appearance of a building. Some minor works are covered by Permitted Development rights.

Works that usually NEED planning permission:
- Building a new house or flat
- Extensions beyond permitted development limits
- Changing the use of a building (e.g. house to flats, shop to restaurant)
- Building in a conservation area or on listed buildings
- Installing large solar panels facing a road
- Building a wall or fence over 2 metres high

Works that usually DO NOT need planning permission (Permitted Development):
- Small rear extensions (up to 3m for semi-detached, 4m for detached since 2019)
- Loft conversions within certain limits
- Internal alterations (unless listed building)
- Garden sheds and outbuildings under 2.5m height
- Fences under 2 metres (1 metre next to a highway)
- Solar panels (not on a listed building)

How to Apply
1. Check if you need permission: Use our online tool or speak to our planning team
2. Pre-application advice: We offer paid pre-application advice (150 pounds for householder, 500 pounds for major). Book at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/planning
3. Submit your application: Apply online at www.planningportal.co.uk
4. Application fees: Householder applications 258 pounds; full applications vary by type

What Happens Next
- Validation: We check your application is complete (5 working days)
- Consultation: Neighbours and statutory consultees are notified (21 days)
- Assessment: Planning officer assesses against local and national policy
- Decision: Most applications decided within 8 weeks (householder) or 13 weeks (major)
- You will receive a decision notice by email

Planning Enforcement
If you believe someone has carried out work without planning permission, report it to us. We investigate all reports. Call 01234-567-892 or email planning.enforcement@samplecouncil.gov.uk.

Building Control
Building regulations approval is separate from planning permission. You may need both. Contact building.control@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-893.
