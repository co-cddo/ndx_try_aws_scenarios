SAMPLE COUNCIL - COMMUNITY SAFETY GUIDE

Anti-Social Behaviour (ASB)
Report ASB online at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/report-asb or call 01234-567-880.
Types of ASB we deal with: noise nuisance, harassment, vandalism, fly-tipping, abandoned vehicles, drug dealing, intimidation.
We work with the police and housing associations to tackle persistent ASB using Community Protection Notices and injunctions.

Domestic Abuse
If you or someone you know is experiencing domestic abuse:
National helpline (24hr): 0808 2000 247
Local support: Sample Refuge 01234-567-881
In immediate danger: Call 999
We can help with emergency housing, legal support, and safety planning.

CCTV
We operate 150 CCTV cameras across the town centre, car parks, and key locations. Monitored 24 hours a day 7 days a week. Subject Access Requests for footage must be made within 30 days.

Neighbourhood Watch
Join or start a Neighbourhood Watch scheme. Contact community.safety@samplecouncil.gov.uk for support and resources.

Hate Crime
Report hate crime to the police on 101 (or 999 in emergency). Third-party reporting is available through our offices or online. All reports are taken seriously.

Contact: community.safety@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-880
