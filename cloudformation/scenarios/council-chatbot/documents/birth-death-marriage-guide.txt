SAMPLE COUNCIL - REGISTRATION SERVICES (BIRTHS, DEATHS, MARRIAGES)

Registering a Birth
Births must be registered within 42 days. Book an appointment at the Register Office.
Both parents' details can be included if married. Unmarried parents must both attend to include the father's details.
Register Office: Civic Centre, High Street, SM1 1AA
Appointments: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/register-birth or 01234-567-865

Registering a Death
Deaths must be registered within 5 days (unless referred to the coroner). Book an appointment.
Bring: medical certificate of cause of death, deceased's birth certificate, NHS card, and marriage certificate if applicable.
Tell Us Once service available to notify multiple government departments in a single call.

Marriage and Civil Partnership
Notice of marriage: Both parties must give notice at their local register office at least 28 days before the ceremony.
Ceremony venues: Register Office (165 pounds), approved venues across the borough (fees vary).
Citizenship ceremonies: Held on the third Thursday of each month at the Civic Centre.

Certificates
Copies of birth, death, and marriage certificates: 11 pounds each (standard delivery 7 working days) or 35 pounds for priority (next working day).

Contact: registrars@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-865
