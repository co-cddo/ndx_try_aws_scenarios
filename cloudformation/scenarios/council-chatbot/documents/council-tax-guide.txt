SAMPLE COUNCIL - COUNCIL TAX GUIDE 2024-25

What is Council Tax?
Council tax is a local tax on domestic properties to fund local services including schools, police, fire service, roads, libraries, and waste collection.

Council Tax Bands and Charges 2024-25
Band A: 1,198.42 pounds (properties valued up to 40,000 pounds in 1991)
Band B: 1,398.16 pounds (40,001 to 52,000 pounds)
Band C: 1,597.90 pounds (52,001 to 68,000 pounds)
Band D: 1,797.63 pounds (68,001 to 88,000 pounds)
Band E: 2,197.10 pounds (88,001 to 120,000 pounds)
Band F: 2,596.58 pounds (120,001 to 160,000 pounds)
Band G: 2,996.05 pounds (160,001 to 320,000 pounds)
Band H: 3,595.26 pounds (over 320,000 pounds)

How to Pay
- Direct Debit: Set up online or by phone. Pay over 10 or 12 monthly instalments.
- Online: Pay by debit or credit card at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/pay
- Phone: Call 01234-567-891 (automated 24-hour line)
- Bank transfer: Sort code 12-34-56, Account 12345678, Reference: your council tax account number
- Post Office or PayPoint: Take your bill to any branch

Discounts and Exemptions
- Single Person Discount: 25% reduction if only one adult lives in the property. Apply online or by phone.
- Student Exemption: Properties occupied only by full-time students are exempt. Provide student certificates from your institution.
- Disabled Band Reduction: If your property has been adapted for a disabled person, you may qualify for a reduction to the next lower band.
- Empty Properties: A 100% charge applies to unfurnished empty properties after 2 years, and a 200% premium after 5 years.
- Second Homes: 100% council tax premium applies from April 2025.

Council Tax Support (Reduction)
If you are on a low income or receiving benefits, you may qualify for council tax support of up to 100%. Apply online at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/cts or collect a form from the council offices.

Moving Home
Tell us when you move so we can adjust your bill. You are liable for council tax at your new address from the date you move in. Contact us within 21 days.

Challenging Your Band
If you believe your property is in the wrong band, contact the Valuation Office Agency (VOA) at www.voa.gov.uk.

Contact: counciltax@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-891
