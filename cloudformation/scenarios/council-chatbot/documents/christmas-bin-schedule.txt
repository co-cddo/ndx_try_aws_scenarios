SAMPLE COUNCIL - CHRISTMAS AND NEW YEAR BIN COLLECTION SCHEDULE

Revised Collection Dates
If your normal collection day falls on a bank holiday, your collection will be delayed:
Wednesday 25 December (Christmas Day): Collected Saturday 28 December
Thursday 26 December (Boxing Day): Collected Sunday 29 December
Wednesday 1 January (New Year's Day): Collected Saturday 4 January
All other collection days remain unchanged. Normal schedules resume from Monday 6 January.

Extra Recycling
We expect more recycling over the Christmas period. If your blue bin is full:
Flatten all cardboard boxes.
Place extra recycling in clear bags next to your blue bin (Christmas period only).
Remove all wrapping paper that is NOT recyclable (metallic, glitter, or plastic-coated paper goes in the black bin).

Real Christmas Trees
Free collection service available in January. Register at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/tree-collection by 10 January. Trees must be placed at the kerbside on your normal collection day. Remove all decorations and pot.
The Household Waste Recycling Centre is closed on 25-26 December and 1 January only.

Contact: waste@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-890
