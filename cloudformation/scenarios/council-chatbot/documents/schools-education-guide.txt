SAMPLE COUNCIL - SCHOOLS AND EDUCATION GUIDE

School Admissions
Apply for school places at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/school-admissions or call 01234-567-860.
Primary school (Reception year): Apply by 15 January, offers made on 16 April.
Secondary school (Year 7): Apply by 31 October, offers made on 1 March.
In-year transfers: Apply at any time, decisions within 15 school days.

Schools in the Borough
We have 24 primary schools, 5 secondary schools, and 1 special school. All school performance data and Ofsted reports are available at www.gov.uk/school-performance-tables.

Special Educational Needs and Disabilities (SEND)
If your child has additional needs, contact our SEND team: send@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-861.
Education, Health and Care (EHC) plans: Apply via the SEND team. Assessment takes up to 20 weeks.

School Transport
Free transport for primary pupils living more than 2 miles from their nearest school, and secondary pupils living more than 3 miles. Additional support for SEND pupils.
Apply: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/school-transport

Free School Meals
Children whose parents receive Universal Credit (with net earnings under 7,400 pounds) or other qualifying benefits are eligible. Apply online.

Contact: education@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-860
