SAMPLE COUNCIL - HOUSING REPAIRS FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: I have no heating or hot water - is this an emergency?
A: Yes, between October and April this is treated as an emergency repair. Call 01234-567-800 (24 hours). Between May and September, it is treated as urgent (3 working days).

Q: Who is responsible for repairs - me or the council?
A: The council is responsible for the structure, exterior, plumbing, electrical wiring, heating systems, and communal areas. You are responsible for internal decoration, minor fixtures (curtain rails, shelves), and replacing light bulbs.

Q: How long will my repair take?
A: Emergency: 2-4 hours. Urgent: 3 working days. Routine: 20 working days. Complex repairs requiring parts may take longer.

Q: Can I do my own repairs?
A: Yes for minor work such as painting and shelving. For gas, electrical, or structural work, you must use the council's approved contractors. Unauthorised alterations may affect your tenancy.

Q: My window is broken - will the council fix it?
A: If the damage is due to wear and tear or an attempted break-in, yes. If you or your household caused the damage, you may be charged for the repair.

Contact: repairs@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-800
