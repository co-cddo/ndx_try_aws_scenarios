SAMPLE COUNCIL - LIBRARIES AND LEISURE GUIDE

Libraries
We have 6 libraries across the borough. Library membership is free for all residents.

Central Library, High Street, SM1 1BB
Mon-Fri 9am-7pm, Sat 9am-5pm, Sun 11am-4pm
Services: books, DVDs, free WiFi, public computers, printing (10p/page), meeting rooms, children's activities.

Branch Libraries (5 locations):
Mon, Wed, Fri 10am-5pm, Tue, Thu 10am-7pm, Sat 10am-2pm
Locations: Northside, Southgate, East Park, Westfield, Hillcrest

Online Services: eBooks and eAudiobooks free via the BorrowBox app. Online reference databases available 24/7.

Events: Rhyme Time (weekly, under-5s), Code Club (weekly, ages 8-12), Reading Groups (monthly, adults), Digital Skills Drop-in (weekly).

Parks and Open Spaces
We maintain 15 parks and 8 play areas across the borough.
Victoria Park (SM1 3CC): Playground, skate park, tennis courts, bowling green, cafe.
Riverside Walk: 3km riverside path, accessible for wheelchairs and pushchairs.
Allotments: Apply for an allotment plot (waiting list approximately 2 years). Annual rent 50-100 pounds depending on plot size.

Leisure Centres
Sample Leisure Centre, Sports Way, SM1 4DD
Facilities: 25m pool, gym, sports hall, squash courts, studio.
Membership: 35 pounds/month (adult), 20 pounds/month (concession), under-16s free with paying adult.
Pay-as-you-go: Swim 5 pounds, gym 7 pounds, class 6 pounds.

Contact: libraries@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-870
