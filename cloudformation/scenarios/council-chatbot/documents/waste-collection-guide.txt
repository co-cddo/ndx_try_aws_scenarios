SAMPLE COUNCIL - WASTE COLLECTION AND RECYCLING GUIDE

Bin Collection Schedule
Your bins are collected on a regular schedule based on your area. To find your exact collection day, enter your postcode on our website or contact us.

General Waste (Black Bin) - Collected WEEKLY every Monday
Place your black bin at the kerbside by 7am on collection day. The bin must have the lid fully closed. We will not collect overflowing bins.
Accepted: general household waste, nappies, pet waste (bagged), broken crockery, vacuum cleaner contents.
Not accepted: garden waste, electrical items, batteries, paint, chemicals, building rubble.

Recycling (Blue Bin) - Collected FORTNIGHTLY on Thursdays (odd weeks)
Clean and dry recyclables only. Do not bag items - place them loose in the bin.
Accepted: paper, cardboard (flattened), plastic bottles and tubs, tin cans, aluminium foil (clean), glass bottles and jars, aerosol cans (empty), food and drink cartons.
Not accepted: plastic bags, cling film, polystyrene, nappies, food waste, textiles, electrical items.

Garden Waste (Green Bin) - Collected FORTNIGHTLY on Thursdays (even weeks)
This is an opt-in service costing 35 pounds per year. Register on our website or call 01234-567-890.
Accepted: grass cuttings, hedge trimmings, weeds, leaves, small branches (under 10cm diameter), flowers, plants.
Not accepted: soil, stones, treated wood, animal waste, food waste, plastic pots.

Bulky Waste Collection
We collect large household items for a fee. Book online or by phone.
- 1-3 items: 25 pounds
- 4-6 items: 45 pounds
Items collected: furniture, mattresses, carpets, appliances (not fridges/freezers).

Household Waste Recycling Centre
Location: Riverside Industrial Estate, Sample Road, SM1 2AB
Opening hours: Monday to Saturday 8am-6pm, Sunday 9am-4pm
Proof of address required. Cars and small vans only (permit needed for larger vehicles).

Missed Collections
Report a missed collection within 2 working days. We will return within 3 working days.
Phone: 01234-567-890
Online: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/missed-collection

Christmas Collection Changes
Collections may change during the Christmas period. Revised schedules are published in December on our website and in the local press.

Contaminated Bins
If your recycling bin contains non-recyclable items, we will leave a tag explaining why it was not collected. Remove the contamination and present the bin on your next collection day.

Contact: waste@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-890
