SAMPLE COUNCIL - PARKS AND ALLOTMENTS DETAILED GUIDE

Our Parks
We maintain 15 parks across the borough, from small pocket parks to Victoria Park (20 acres).
All parks are open from dawn to dusk. No entry fee.

Victoria Park (SM1 3CC)
Our flagship park with playground, skate park, tennis courts (book online, 6 pounds per hour), bowling green, bandstand, and cafe. Friends of Victoria Park volunteer group meets monthly.

Play Areas
8 equipped play areas for ages 2-14. Inspected weekly. Report damaged equipment online or call 01234-567-870.

Allotments
We manage 4 allotment sites with 200 plots total.
Full plot (250 sq m): 65 pounds per year.
Half plot (125 sq m): 35 pounds per year.
Concession rate (over 65 or registered disabled): 50 percent discount.
Waiting list: Apply at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/allotments. Average wait approximately 2 years.
Plots not cultivated for 6 months may be reclaimed.

Tree Planting
We plant approximately 200 new trees per year. Request a tree for your street at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/tree-request.

Contact: parks@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-870
