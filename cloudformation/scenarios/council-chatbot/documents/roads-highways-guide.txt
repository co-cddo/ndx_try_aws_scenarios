SAMPLE COUNCIL - ROADS AND HIGHWAYS GUIDE

Report a Pothole
Report potholes online at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/potholes or call 01234-567-895.
Response times:
- Emergency (danger to life, major road): 2 hours
- Category 1 (significant defect on main road): 24 hours
- Category 2 (defect on main road): 5 working days
- Category 3 (minor defect or residential road): 28 days
We inspect all reports and prioritise based on risk. We repair approximately 5,000 potholes per year.

Street Lighting
Report a faulty street light online or by phone. Include the lamp column number (on the column near the base).
- Light not working: Repaired within 5 working days
- Light on during the day: Repaired within 10 working days
- Damaged column (e.g. vehicle collision): Emergency response within 2 hours

Winter Gritting
We grit priority routes (A and B roads, bus routes, steep hills) when temperatures are forecast to drop below zero.
Grit bins are located on steep residential streets. Report empty grit bins online.
Priority route map: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/gritting-routes

Road Closures and Roadworks
Check current and planned roadworks at www.one.network or www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/roadworks.
Apply for a temporary road closure: Contact highways@samplecouncil.gov.uk (minimum 12 weeks notice).

Pavement Maintenance
Report damaged or uneven pavements online. We inspect and repair based on the same categories as potholes.
Tree root damage: We work with our tree team to minimise root damage while preserving trees where possible.

Flooding and Drainage
Report blocked drains or flooding online or by phone. Emergency flooding: call 01234-567-895 (24 hours).
We are the Lead Local Flood Authority and maintain a flood risk register.

Contact: highways@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-895
