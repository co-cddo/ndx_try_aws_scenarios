SAMPLE COUNCIL - CHILDREN'S SERVICES GUIDE

Early Years and Childcare
Free childcare: 15 hours per week for all 3-4 year olds. 30 hours for eligible working parents.
2-year-old funding: Free 15 hours for families on low incomes or qualifying benefits.
Find childcare: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/childcare-search

Children's Centres
Five centres across the borough offering parenting support, health visitor sessions, stay-and-play groups, and adult learning. All services are free.

Safeguarding Children
If you are concerned about a child's safety or welfare:
During office hours: 01234-567-840 (Children's Social Care)
Out of hours: 01234-567-802 (Emergency Duty Team)
In immediate danger: Call 999
All concerns are treated confidentially and investigated by qualified social workers.

Fostering and Adoption
We urgently need foster carers, especially for teenagers and sibling groups.
Fostering enquiries: 01234-567-841 or fostering@samplecouncil.gov.uk
Adoption enquiries: 01234-567-842 or adoption@samplecouncil.gov.uk

Youth Services
Youth clubs for ages 11-19 at three locations. Activities include sports, arts, music, and mentoring.

Contact: childrens.services@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-840
