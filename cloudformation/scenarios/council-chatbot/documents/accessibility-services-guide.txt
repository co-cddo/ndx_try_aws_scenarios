SAMPLE COUNCIL - ACCESSIBILITY AND INCLUSIVE SERVICES

Our Commitment
We are committed to making all council services accessible. We hold the Disability Confident Leader accreditation.

Communication
Documents available in large print, audio, Braille, and Easy Read on request.
British Sign Language (BSL) interpretation available for appointments (book 5 working days in advance).
Telephone relay service (TextRelay): Dial 18001 followed by the council number.
Language interpretation available for over 100 languages.

Physical Access
All council buildings have step-free access, accessible toilets, and hearing loops.
Blue Badge parking spaces at all council buildings.
Changing Places facility at the Civic Centre and Sample Leisure Centre.

Online Access
Our website meets WCAG 2.1 AA standards. Report accessibility issues to webteam@samplecouncil.gov.uk.
Screen reader compatible forms available for all online services.

Disabled Facilities Grants
Grants up to 30,000 pounds for home adaptations (ramps, stairlifts, wet rooms, door widening). Means-tested for adults, not means-tested for children.
Apply: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/dfg or call 01234-567-850.

Contact: accessibility@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-890
