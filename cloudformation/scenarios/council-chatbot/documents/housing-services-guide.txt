SAMPLE COUNCIL - HOUSING SERVICES GUIDE

Council Housing
We manage approximately 8,500 council homes across the borough. Our tenants benefit from secure tenancies, affordable rents, and a responsive repairs service.

Housing Register (Waiting List)
To apply for council housing, you must join the housing register. Applications are assessed using a banding system:
- Band A (Emergency): Fleeing domestic abuse, severe overcrowding, demolition orders
- Band B (High Priority): Homeless households, medical needs requiring a move, overcrowded by 2+ bedrooms
- Band C (Medium Priority): Overcrowded by 1 bedroom, sharing facilities
- Band D (Low Priority): Adequately housed but wanting to move
Apply online at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/housing-register. Average waiting time varies by band and property size (Band B: 12-18 months for a 2-bed; Band D: 3-5 years).

Reporting Repairs
Emergency repairs (24-hour): 01234-567-800 (gas leaks, burst pipes, no heating in winter, broken external door locks)
Urgent repairs (3 working days): Partial heating failure, toilet not flushing, leaking roof
Routine repairs (20 working days): Dripping taps, broken cupboard doors, cracked tiles
Report online: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/repairs

Homelessness and Housing Advice
If you are homeless or at risk of homelessness, contact us immediately. We have a legal duty to help.
- Housing Options Team: 01234-567-801 (Mon-Fri 9am-5pm)
- Out of hours emergency: 01234-567-802
- Drop-in: Housing Centre, High Street, SM1 1AA (Mon-Fri 9am-4pm, no appointment needed)

Right to Buy
If you have been a council tenant for 3+ years, you may be eligible to buy your home at a discount (up to 87,200 pounds for houses, 116,200 pounds for flats in 2024-25). Contact us for an application form.

Private Sector Housing
We inspect private rented properties for hazards and can take enforcement action against landlords who fail to maintain safe conditions.

Contact: housing@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-801
