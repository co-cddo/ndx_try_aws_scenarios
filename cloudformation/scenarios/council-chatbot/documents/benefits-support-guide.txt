SAMPLE COUNCIL - BENEFITS AND SUPPORT GUIDE

Housing Benefit
Housing Benefit helps pay rent if you are on a low income. It is being replaced by Universal Credit for most new claims, but you may still be eligible if you are pension age or living in supported housing.
Apply: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/housing-benefit or collect a form from council offices.
Processing time: New claims 20 working days, changes 10 working days.

Council Tax Support
You may be eligible for council tax support if you are on a low income or receiving certain benefits.
- Working age: Up to 80% reduction (you always pay at least 20%)
- Pension age: Up to 100% reduction
Apply online: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/council-tax-support

Discretionary Housing Payments
If Housing Benefit or Universal Credit housing element does not cover your full rent, you may apply for additional help. Limited fund available.

Free School Meals
Children whose parents receive certain benefits qualify for free school meals. Apply online at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/free-school-meals.

Local Welfare Assistance
Emergency help for residents in crisis. We can provide food bank vouchers, emergency fuel payments, and essential household items.
Contact: 01234-567-898 (Mon-Fri 9am-5pm)

Cost of Living Support
Visit our Cost of Living Hub: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/cost-of-living for information about energy grants, food support, debt advice, and benefit calculators.

Warm Homes Scheme
Free home insulation and heating improvements for eligible residents (owner-occupiers on low incomes or receiving certain benefits).

Contact: benefits@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-898
