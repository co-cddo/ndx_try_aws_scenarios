SAMPLE COUNCIL - PARKING SERVICES GUIDE

Resident Parking Permits
Annual permits are available for residents in controlled parking zones:
- First permit: 50 pounds per year
- Second permit: 100 pounds per year
- Third permit: Not available (maximum 2 per household)
- Visitor permits: 1 pound per day (book of 20 for 20 pounds)
Apply online: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/parking-permits
Processing time: 5-10 working days

Council Car Parks
Town Centre Multi-Storey (200 spaces): Up to 1 hour 1.50 pounds, up to 3 hours 3.50 pounds, all day 7 pounds
High Street Surface Car Park (80 spaces): Up to 1 hour 1 pound, up to 3 hours 2.50 pounds, all day 5 pounds
Railway Station Car Park (150 spaces): All day 8 pounds, season ticket 120 pounds/month
Free parking after 6pm and all day Sunday in all council car parks.
Pay by phone: Download the RingGo app or call 01onal number on the machine.

Penalty Charge Notices (PCNs)
If you receive a PCN, you have 28 days to pay or challenge it.
- Higher rate (e.g. parking on double yellow lines): 70 pounds (35 pounds if paid within 14 days)
- Lower rate (e.g. expired ticket): 50 pounds (25 pounds if paid within 14 days)
Challenge a PCN: Write to parking.challenges@samplecouncil.gov.uk within 28 days with evidence.

Blue Badge Holders
Blue badge holders can park free in all council car parks and on single yellow lines for up to 3 hours.
Apply for a blue badge: www.gov.uk/apply-blue-badge or visit our offices with supporting documentation.

Dropped Kerbs
To request a vehicle crossover (dropped kerb) for your driveway: apply online (250 pound inspection fee, construction costs vary).

Contact: parking@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-894
