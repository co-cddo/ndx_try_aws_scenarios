SAMPLE COUNCIL - COUNCIL MEETINGS AND DEMOCRACY

Council Meetings
Full Council meets on the first Wednesday of every month at 7pm in the Council Chamber, Civic Centre.
Cabinet meets on the third Tuesday of every month at 6pm.
Planning Committee meets on the second Thursday of every month at 6pm.
All meetings are open to the public. Agendas published 5 working days before each meeting.

Public Questions
Members of the public can ask questions at Full Council meetings. Submit questions at least 3 working days before the meeting to democratic.services@samplecouncil.gov.uk.

Petitions
Submit petitions with 25 or more signatures to trigger a council response. Petitions with 1,000 or more signatures trigger a Full Council debate.
Submit online: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/petitions

Your Councillors
The borough has 36 councillors representing 12 wards (3 per ward). Find your councillor at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/find-councillor.
Councillor surgeries are held regularly in each ward. Check your councillor's page for times and locations.

Freedom of Information
Submit FOI requests at foi@samplecouncil.gov.uk. Response within 20 working days.

Contact: democratic.services@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-866
