SAMPLE COUNCIL - SUMMER ACTIVITIES AND EVENTS GUIDE

Summer Holiday Activities for Children
Our leisure centres and parks offer activities during the school holidays (late July to early September):
Sports camps (ages 5-14): Multi-sport, football, tennis, swimming. Book at the leisure centre.
Play schemes (ages 5-11): Free play sessions in parks and community centres.
Holiday Activity and Food programme (HAF): Free for children receiving free school meals. Register at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/haf.

Outdoor Events
Music in the Park: Free concerts every Sunday in July and August at Victoria Park bandstand.
Summer Reading Challenge: Free for all children at our libraries. Complete the challenge to earn a medal and certificate.
Outdoor cinema: Two screenings per month in Victoria Park. Tickets 5 pounds.

Parks and Play Areas
All 15 parks are open from dawn to dusk. Paddling pools at Victoria Park and East Park are open 10am-5pm daily in July and August (weather permitting).

Swimming
Outdoor swim sessions at Sample Leisure Centre lido: May to September. Adult 5 pounds, child 3 pounds, family (2 adults and 2 children) 12 pounds.

Contact: events@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-870
