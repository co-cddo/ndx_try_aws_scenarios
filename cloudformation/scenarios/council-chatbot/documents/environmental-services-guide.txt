SAMPLE COUNCIL - ENVIRONMENTAL SERVICES GUIDE

Noise Complaints
Report noise nuisance (loud music, barking dogs, construction outside permitted hours, alarms):
- During office hours: 01234-567-896
- Out of hours noise service (Fri-Sat 8pm-3am): 01234-567-896 option 2
- Online diary: Record noise disturbances at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/noise-diary
Construction noise is permitted Monday-Friday 8am-6pm, Saturday 8am-1pm. No construction noise on Sundays or bank holidays.

Pest Control
We offer subsidised pest control for council tenants (free for rats and mice, 50 pounds for other pests).
Private residents: Treatment from 75 pounds depending on pest type.
Pests treated: rats, mice, wasps, cockroaches, bedbugs, fleas.
Book: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/pest-control or 01234-567-896

Fly-Tipping
Report fly-tipping (illegal dumping) online or by phone. Include photos and location details if possible.
We investigate all reports and prosecute offenders where evidence is available. Fines up to 50,000 pounds or imprisonment.
Fixed penalty notice for small-scale fly-tipping: 400 pounds.
Report: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/fly-tipping or 01234-567-896

Air Quality
We monitor air quality across the borough. Live data available at www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/air-quality.
Air Quality Management Areas (AQMAs) are declared where pollution exceeds national standards.

Food Safety
We inspect all food businesses. Ratings are published at www.food.gov.uk/ratings.
Report a food safety concern: 01234-567-896 or environmental.health@samplecouncil.gov.uk

Contact: environmental.health@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-896
