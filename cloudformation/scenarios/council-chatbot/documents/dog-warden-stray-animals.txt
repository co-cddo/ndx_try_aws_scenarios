SAMPLE COUNCIL - DOG WARDEN AND STRAY ANIMALS

Stray Dogs
If you find a stray dog, contact the Dog Warden: 01234-567-875 (Mon-Fri 8am-5pm).
Out of hours strays: Call 01234-567-999 (emergency line).
Stray dogs are held for 7 days. If unclaimed, they are rehomed through local rescue partners.
Collection fee for reclaimed dogs: 75 pounds plus 15 pounds per day kennelling.

Dog Fouling
Dog fouling is an offence. Fixed penalty: 100 pounds. Report persistent fouling hotspots online.
Dog waste bins are located in all parks. If a dog waste bin is full, use any litter bin.
All dog walkers must carry bags and clean up after their dogs in public areas.

Dangerous Dogs
Report dangerous or out-of-control dogs to the police on 101 (999 if immediate danger).
Banned breeds under the Dangerous Dogs Act: Pit Bull Terrier, Japanese Tosa, Dogo Argentino, Fila Brasileiro.

Microchipping
All dogs over 8 weeks old must be microchipped by law. Free microchipping events are held quarterly at council offices.

Dog Control Orders
Dogs must be kept on leads in cemeteries, playing fields, and marked areas in parks.
Dogs are excluded from children's play areas and formal gardens.

Contact: dogwarden@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-875
