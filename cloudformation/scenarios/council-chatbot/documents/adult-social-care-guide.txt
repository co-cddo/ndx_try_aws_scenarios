SAMPLE COUNCIL - ADULT SOCIAL CARE GUIDE

If you or someone you care for needs help with daily living, we can assess your needs and arrange support.

Requesting an Assessment
Anyone can request a care needs assessment. Call 01234-567-850 or visit www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/adult-care.
Assessments are free and usually completed within 28 days.

Types of Support Available
Home care: Help with washing, dressing, meals, and medication.
Day centres: Social activities and respite for carers.
Residential care: Full-time care in a care home.
Supported living: Help to live independently in your own home.
Equipment and adaptations: Grab rails, stairlifts, level-access showers.
Direct payments: Money to arrange your own care.

Paying for Care
If your savings are above 23,250 pounds, you will pay the full cost of care.
Between 14,250 and 23,250 pounds, you contribute from your savings.
Below 14,250 pounds, you pay from your income only (assessed individually).

Carers Support
If you care for someone, you are entitled to a carer's assessment. We can provide respite care, support groups, and emergency planning.
Carers helpline: 01234-567-851

Safeguarding
If you are concerned about an adult at risk of abuse or neglect, call 01234-567-850 immediately. In an emergency, call 999.

Contact: adult.care@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-850
