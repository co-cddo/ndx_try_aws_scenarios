SAMPLE COUNCIL - SEVERE WEATHER GUIDANCE

Snow and Ice
Priority gritting routes are treated when temperatures drop below zero. Map: www.samplecouncil.gov.uk/gritting-routes.
Report dangerous ice on pavements: 01234-567-895.
Grit bins on steep residential streets are refilled regularly during cold spells.
School closures are announced on our website and social media by 7:30am.

Heatwave Advice
Check on elderly and vulnerable neighbours.
Cool Centres open during heatwaves at libraries and community centres (air-conditioned).
Report concerns about vulnerable people: 01234-567-850.
Avoid outdoor exercise between 11am and 3pm during extreme heat.

High Winds and Storms
Secure loose items in gardens (trampolines, bins, furniture).
Report fallen trees blocking roads: 01234-567-895 (priority clearance within 2 hours for main roads).
Report damaged buildings: 01234-567-999 (out of hours emergency).
Power cuts: Contact your electricity provider or call 105.

Cancelled Services
During severe weather, some services may be affected including bin collections, street cleaning, and outdoor events. Check our website and social media for updates.

Contact: highways@samplecouncil.gov.uk or 01234-567-895
